General

What is Calyvon AI?

Calyvon AI is an all-in-one growth platform built specifically for local service businesses. We combine an AI Employee that handles every lead 24/7, digital marketing, reputation management, and automation — so your business grows without you having to work more hours.

What types of businesses do you work with?

We work with local service businesses across home services (plumbing, HVAC, electrical, roofing, landscaping), legal and professional services (personal injury, family law, accounting), and other local businesses like dental offices, auto repair shops, fitness studios, and restaurants.

Is there a long-term contract?

Never. No contracts, no lock-in and no cancellation fees. We keep clients by delivering real results not by trapping them in agreements. Cancel anytime with zero penalties.

What if it does not work for my business?

If you do not book at least 3 confirmed leads in your first 90 days we refund every penny. No questions asked. We call it the Never Miss a Lead Guarantee and we stand behind it completely.

AI Employee

Does the AI actually sound like a real person?

Yes. Your AI Employee is trained specifically on your business — your services, prices, service area and tone. Customers interact with it naturally and comfortably. It does not sound robotic. It sounds like someone who knows your business inside out because it does.

How long does setup take?

Most businesses are fully live within 48 hours. We handle the entire setup including your phone number, website chat, Facebook and Instagram. You do not touch a single line of code.

What platforms and channels does it work on?

Phone calls, SMS, website chat, Facebook Messenger and Instagram DMs — all landing in one unified inbox on your mobile app so nothing ever falls through the cracks.

How is this different from a call center?

A call center charges per call, needs constant training, takes sick days and clocks out at 5pm. Your Calyvon AI Employee costs one flat monthly fee, knows your exact business, responds in under 10 seconds and works 24 hours a day 7 days a week 365 days a year without ever missing a beat.

Can the AI book appointments directly into my calendar?

Yes. The AI Employee integrates directly with your booking calendar and can schedule, confirm, and send reminders for appointments — all without human intervention. You see new bookings land in real time.

Marketing

What digital marketing services do you offer?

We run Google Ads, Meta (Facebook and Instagram) Ads, local SEO, Google Business Profile optimization, and landing page management. Everything is managed by our team — you do not need to learn any ad platform yourself.

How do you help with reviews and reputation?

After every job, we automatically send a review request to your customer via SMS. Our AI monitors and responds to all reviews across Google, Facebook, and Yelp — protecting your brand and building a 5-star reputation on autopilot.

How quickly will I see results?

Most clients see new leads within the first week. Paid advertising delivers results fastest. SEO and reputation build over 30–90 days. Our 90-day guarantee is designed around this timeline.

Pricing

How much does Calyvon AI cost?

Pricing is based on your business size and which services you need. We offer flat monthly plans with no hidden fees and no per-lead charges. Book a free strategy call and we will give you an exact number in 15 minutes.

Is there a setup fee?

There is a one-time setup fee of $497 that covers your full onboarding, AI training, and integration. We regularly run promotions to waive this fee — check the homepage for the current offer.

Technical

Do I need to install anything or know how to code?

No. We handle 100% of the technical setup. You receive login credentials to a mobile app where you can see all your conversations, leads, and analytics. No code, no servers, no technical knowledge required.

Does it integrate with my existing CRM or software?

Yes. We integrate with most major CRMs and scheduling tools including ServiceTitan, Jobber, HousecallPro, and hundreds more via Zapier. Our team handles the integration for you during onboarding.

Is my data and my customers' data secure?

Yes. We are SOC 2 Type II certified, hosted on AWS infrastructure, and fully GDPR compliant. All customer data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell or share your data with third parties.
